Christchurch · Metal Finishing

Our services

Surface prep, plating and paint removal for metal parts of every size. Not sure what your part needs? Send a photo and we'll point you the right way.

01

Zinc Plating

Bright and yellow-passivate zinc for brackets, fasteners, fittings and fabricated steel that needs everyday corrosion protection.

02

Chrome Plating

Decorative bright chrome for bumpers, trim, grilles and show parts. Even, mirror-bright coverage over a properly prepared base.

03

Nickel Plating

Durable nickel as a hard-wearing finish in its own right, or as the base layer that gives chrome its depth and shine.

04

Chemical Paint Stripping

Dip stripping lifts old paint, powder coat and primer off metal without the heat or abrasion damage of blasting — back to clean bare steel.

05

Rust & Scale Removal

Derusting baths clear corrosion and mill scale from older parts so they're sound and ready to plate, paint or powder coat.

06

Restoration Refinishing

Strip-and-replate work for vintage and classic parts, with careful handling of delicate castings, trim, hardware and one-offs.
