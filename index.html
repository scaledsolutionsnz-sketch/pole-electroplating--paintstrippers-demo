What We Do

Plating & stripping services

Surface prep, plating and paint removal for metal parts of all sizes. Below are the services we're commonly asked for — tell us what you've got and we'll advise the right process.

01

Zinc Plating

Bright and yellow-passivate zinc for brackets, fasteners, fittings and fabricated parts that need corrosion protection.

02

Chrome Plating

Decorative bright chrome for bumpers, trim, grilles and show-quality restoration parts — mirror finish, evenly laid.

03

Copper & Nickel Plating

Copper and hard-wearing nickel — a durable finish in its own right, or the base layer that gives chrome its depth and shine.

04

Electropolishing

Electrochemical polishing that smooths, brightens and deburrs stainless and other metals for a clean, even, corrosion-resistant finish.

05

Chemical Paint Stripping

Dip stripping that lifts old paint, powder coat and primer off metal without sandblasting damage — back to clean bare steel.

06

Rust & Scale Removal

Derusting baths that clear corrosion and mill scale from older parts so they're sound and ready to plate or paint.

07

Reconditioning & Repairs

Strip, repair and replate work for vintage, classic and one-off parts — careful handling of delicate castings, trim and hardware.

About the Workshop

A Christchurch metal finishing shop that treats your parts like its own

Pole Electroplating & Paintstrippers handles the messy, exacting end of metalwork — getting old coatings off and fresh plating on, so your parts come back clean, protected and looking right.

Whether it's a single bumper for a restoration, a batch of brackets off a production line, or a rusty gate that needs stripping back, we work out the right process for the part in front of us and do it properly.

  • Locally owned and operated in Christchurch
  • Clear quotes before any work starts
  • Careful handling of delicate and one-off parts
